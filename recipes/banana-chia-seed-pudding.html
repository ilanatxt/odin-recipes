Banana chia seed pudding

Delicious, refreshing, high in protein and fiber breakfast idea.

For the recipe you'll need:

  • Chia seeds 2tbsp
  • 1 banana
  • Skyr or Greek yogurt 200grams
  • Honey 1tsp
  • Water 100ml
  • Fruits of your own choice for decoration

Preparations:

  1. In a bowl mash banana, then add yogurt, honey and chia seeds. Mix it well.
  2. Add water to your mixture to make it more liquidly. If not added pudding can turn out too thick.
  3. Place your bowl in refrigerator overnight
  4. When ready add your favorite fruit or seeds
