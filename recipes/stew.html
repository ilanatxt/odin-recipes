Warming Stew

DESCRIPTION OF THE RECIPE

This is a warm and delicious recipe of homemade chicken stew with vegetables and sour cream.

Perfect for the colder weather.

For the recipe you'll need:

  • Water 500ml
  • Chicken breast 400grams
  • Potatoes 400grams
  • 1 big carrot
  • 1 big onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of green peas
  • 20% sour cream 200grams
  • Broccoli 200grams
  • Fresh dill and spring onions for garnish
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chicken stock cubes 2pcs

Cooking steps:

  1. Pour water in the pot and put chicken to cook for 10-15min
  2. While chicken cooks,cut onion,carrot,potatoes and broccoli into pieces.You can choose the preferable shape for the veggies
  3. When the chicken becomes white, take it out of the water and leave it for 5min to cool down
  4. Put onion,carrot and potatoes into the pot to cook
  5. Cut chicken into smaller pieces and put it back in the pot with veggies
  6. Put salt, black pepper, 2 chicken stock cubes and sour cream in the pot
  7. Let everything cook for 20-30min,and then add minced garlic,green peas and broccoli in the pot
  8. Let everything cook for another 20-30min on slow burner.Don't forget to mix it every 10min
  9. Add a spoon of sour cream, fresh dill and spring onion when serving
